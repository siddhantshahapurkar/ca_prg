About Us

E-Cell IIT Hyderabad aims to promote entrepreneurship among young minds across India. We aim to create a robust network, through which students can come forward and share ideas among their peers and leading industrialists, by engaging in different events and competitions.

We are launching our CA programme, and you can add value to it. We are looking for students from various colleges across India, who can help us expand this network, and become a part of it. We would like you to be the face of E-Cell IIT Hyderabad in your college and take this initiative forward with your voice and work to make ideas into innovation.

Why CA

An opportunity to expand your network in your campus and outside.

Opportunity to be part of various entrepreneurial events conducted by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad.

Improve your management and communication skills by being a part of CA IIT Hyderabad.

Get access to resources to enhance your knowledge about entrepreneurship and Management.

Certificate of appreciation from E-cell IITH at the end of the tenure.

Terms & Conditions

Students from colleges across the country can participate in this endeavour.

The Campus Ambassadors would be given certain points for each task with a public leader board to ensure Transparency.

All the events must be promoted according to the terms mentioned by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad.

All the events held under this programme would be Supervised by E-Cell IIT Hyderabad directly.

After completion of a task, the CAs must provide the proof to our managers as instructed by them.

In case of any dispute, the final decision lies with E-Cell IITH.

Any case of plagiarism or sharing confidential information would lead to disqualification or may lead to permanent ban in some cases.
